As a freelance statistician/statistical consultant, I was tasked with using data to create a statistical report aimed at the local government and corporate sponsors (in two separate reports). The focus of the report was to statistical analyze the data to explain the benefits of funding the New Orleans Mardi Gras Indian Council's annual event. I used the following statistical methods for this particular case (which can be in the appendix found at the bottom of the reports with more detail): economic and tax roi calculations, statistical significance testing, confidence interval analysis, sensitivity analysis, regression analysis, plots, benchmark comparison methodology, and highly conservative statistical assumptions.
